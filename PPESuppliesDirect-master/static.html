    SCREEN PRINTING
    Uses Pantone colour inks to recreate your design, which are then applied to the garment through mesh screens. Great for large orders.
  •  
    EMBROIDERY
    The design is sewn onto a garment using a computerised embroidery industrial machine to achieve a high quality, long lasting finish.
  •  
    DTG
    The printer directly applies the ink onto the t-shirt with inkjet technology. Direct to Garment Printing is great for complex designs of all colours, small to medium quantities.
  •  
    ECO SOLVENT PRINTING
    Involves ecologically safe printing a coloured design onto vinyl and cutting around the design, which is then heat pressed on to the garment.
  •  
    VINYL
    The plotter cuts out a design onto a single colour sheet of vinyl. The vinyl design is then heat sealed on to the garment using a heat press.

WHAT WE DO
Custom clothing and PPE are important for most businesses. They reduce the risk of injury at work or simply protect from heat or cold. Furthermore, custom clothing is a great tool for building brand awareness while keeping employees safe and comfortable.
That is why we aim to be a world-class business generating long-term value while consistently providing high quality products at a competitive price. Even more, we provide a truly unique customer experience. Products are very easy to find and customise thanks to our customisation tool which is very modern and intuitive.
Choose from an impressive range of products to suit all of you PPE and workwear needs.
Top quality services, fast turnaround times and competitive prices for custom clothing are achieved by using our state of the art printing and embroidery machines. Workwear is customised using the latest technology such as DTG, vinyl or screen printing as well as high volume embroidery.
Furthermore, our design and production teams will provide expert advice on clothing choice or customisation techniques. Moreover, we offer a wide range of services from creating bespoke graphics to digitising your artwork. Therefore, you can rely on us to get the job done right.
We launched as a small business in Kent, UK, focusing on delivering top quality personal protective equipment. We then quickly expanded to cover workwear and other safety solutions. Equally important, we improved every aspect of our business while employing more staff and moving in a larger premises.
We aim to be a one stop shop to meet all your needs.
